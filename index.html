FAQ

For what platform are ambicharts indicators created?
All indicators, free and premium, are built for TradingView. You can use them with your free TradingView account.
Why should I use ambicharts indicators?
ambicharts speeds up chart analysis and gives you a consistent system to see important levels (daily, weekly, monthly opens, highs, lows, horizontals, Fibonacci, VWAP, multi timeframe tools) at a glance, without drawing everything by hand.
Which markets are supported?
Any chart available on TradingView, for example crypto, stocks, FX, indices, commodities.
Do the indicators provide buy or sell signals?
No. They plot technical levels and context only. You decide based on your own plan.
