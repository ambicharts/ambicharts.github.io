Ambi – Daily Chart

Automates the manual charting of your previous day levels (including Fibonacci levels + VWAP Close) and gives you a quick view to see the structure and amplitude of the previous day range.

Features:

  • Percentage label of the previous day range
  • Shading for Pd Body and Pd Wicks
  • Vertical lines to bracket the previous day session
  • Alerts for: DOpen, PdOpen, PdHigh, PdLow, PdEQ

Included levels:

  • DOpen, PdOpen, PdHigh, PdLow, PdEQ
  • Pd Wick Midlines
  • Pd Fibonacci levels
  • Daily VWAP
  • Pd VWAP Closes
