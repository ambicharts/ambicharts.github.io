FAQ

For what platform are ambicharts indicators created?
All indicators, free and premium, are built for TradingView.
Why should I use ambicharts indicators?
ambicharts speeds up chart analysis and gives you a system to see important levels (daily, weekly, monthly opens, highs, lows, horizontals, Fibonacci, VWAP, multi timeframe tools) at a glance, without drawing everything by hand.
Which markets are supported?
Originally created for crypto, but also working for stocks, FX, indices, and commodities.
If you are not sure if the indicators fit your needs, get the 7 days free trial.
Do the indicators provide buy or sell signals?
No. They plot technical levels and context only. You decide based on your own plan.
How can I get them?

Free Lite — TradingView → Indicators → search Ambi_Charts, add the tool you want.

Premium — buy the Ambi Premium guide (120EUR) with detailed instructions for setup, usage, and best-practice workflows. In the guide you will find a voucher for a free 6-month subscription included. You just have to send your TradingView username. Access is granted manually, allow up to 24 hours. After activation, open TradingView → Indicators → Invite Only Scripts.

Can I try the indicators?
Absolutely! Send your TradingView username to hello@ambicharts.com and I will enable premium access manually within 24 hours. If you like it, get the guide with your subscription voucher. If you do not like it, no problem - your free trial will expire automatically.
