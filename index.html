FAQ

For what platform are ambicharts indicators created?

All indicators—free and premium—are built for TradingView. You can use them with a free TradingView account.

Why should I use ambicharts indicators?

ambicharts speeds up chart analysis and gives you a consistent system to see important levels (D/W/M opens/highs/lows, horizontals, Fibs, VWAP, MTF tools) at a glance—without drawing everything by hand.

Which markets are supported?

Any chart available on TradingView: crypto, stocks, FX, indices, commodities, etc.

Do the indicators provide buy/sell signals?

No. They plot technical levels and context only. You make your own decisions based on your plan. Educational only; no financial advice.
